SeniorSplashin'

Objective: Eliminate your assigned target using a water gun. Once you eliminate your target, you inherit their target. Last person standing wins.
How to Play
1
Each player is secretly assigned one target.
2
Eliminate your target with a water gun while following all rules.
3
Once you eliminate your target, you inherit their target and keep playing.
4
Every elimination must be recorded on video and posted to count.
5
The game runs in fortnightly rounds.
⚠ If you do not get a kill during the fortnight, you are eliminated.
6
Avoid getting eliminated. The game ends when one player remains.
Rules
No Eliminations During
  • School hours (8:45am – 3:00pm)
  • Target's Work (while actively working)
  • Assassin's Work (No Eliminating While Working)
  • While driving
Immunity Gear
  • Goggles, floaties etc. grant immunity
  • Must be worn before attacker starts
  • Must be clearly visible
Does not apply during a Purge.
⚡ Bounty
  • A bounty can be placed on any player and is purchasable through the point shop
  • If you have a bounty on your head — no goggles, no safe zones, nothing protects you.
  • Players with a bounty on their head will still be safe during school and while driving
  • Your only option is to evade and not get hit.
Purge Rules
During a Purge —
  • No safe zones
  • No immunity gear (goggles, floaties etc.)
  • ⚠️No eliminations while driving — this rule always applies⚠️
